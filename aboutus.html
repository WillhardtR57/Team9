Our Story

In 2005, long time food lover Esimerkki and her husband Esimerkki, used the balance of their savings and cashed in their
retirement accounts to purchase the Pizza & Kebab, a 30-seat restaurant in Hämeenlinna, Finland. With no restaurant experience, they put it all,
and then some, on the line. Since then they have made there food with love.
